## Clarified component preferences

### PageHeader vs PageHeading

Decision:

```txt
PageHeading is preferred for new Make-generated decision workspaces.
PageHeader remains available for legacy screens that already use it.
```

Updated in:

```txt
contracts/components.contract.json
guidelines/make-minimal-contract.md
guidelines/Guidelines.md
guidelines/setup.md
README.md
```

---

### DetailPanel vs WorkspaceDetailPanel

Decision:

```txt
WorkspaceDetailPanel is preferred for selected-item detail in new decision workspaces.
DetailPanel remains available as a lower-level composition primitive or legacy/static example.
```

Updated in:

```txt
contracts/components.contract.json
guidelines/make-minimal-contract.md
```
