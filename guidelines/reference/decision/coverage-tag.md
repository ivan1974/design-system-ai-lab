# CoverageTag

## Status

v0.7.0 decision primitive.

## Purpose

Use `CoverageTag` to display controlled service coverage values.

It extends the existing badge language instead of creating a local coverage chip.

## Allowed values

- `premium-service-plan-advanced`
- `premium-service-plan-essential`
- `advanced-service-plan`
- `no-coverage`

## Labels

- Premium Service Plan Advanced
- Premium Service Plan Essential
- Advanced Service Plan
- No Coverage

## Rules

- Do not use proprietary service offer names.
- Do not invent new coverage values.
- Third-party assets must use `no-coverage`.
- Do not communicate coverage by color alone.
