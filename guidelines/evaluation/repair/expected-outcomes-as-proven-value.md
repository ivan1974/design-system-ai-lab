# Repair Prompt — Expected Outcomes as Proven Value

## Use when

Use this repair prompt when expected outcomes, estimates or internal proof are shown as proven customer value.

Typical symptoms:

- expected savings shown as delivered value
- avoided downtime shown as proven without proof readiness
- internal activity shown as customer-ready proof
- technical outcome shown as customer value without validation

---

## Repair instruction

```txt
Revise the screen to separate expected outcomes from proven value.

Use expected outcome language for estimates and future benefits.
Do not present expected outcomes as proven value.
Do not present internal proof as customer-ready proof without validation.
Use ValueProofCard when proof readiness, proof points or proof gaps are central.
Show proofReadiness when proof status affects customer use.
Show validationStatus before any customer-ready claim.
Keep human validation visible when proof affects renewal, QBR or customer communication.
```

---

## Acceptance checks

- Expected outcomes are labelled as expected or estimated.
- Internal proof is not shown as customer-ready proof.
- Customer-ready proof has validation context.
- Proof gaps remain visible.
